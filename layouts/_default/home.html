How it Works

1. Tell us your preferences

A brief chat with our advisor helps us understand your needs.

2. Matching Process

Our team will identify a vetted companion based on compatible interests, schedules, location, and other preferences.

3. Email Introduction

Within 4 days, you'll receive an email introduction to a companion carefully vetted and matched to suit you.

4. Meeting

A meeting between you and your chosen potential homesharer to explore compatibility and get acquainted.

5. Ongoing Support & Coordination

Your selected companion provides 15-20 hours of companionship and help with chores each week. Enjoy peace of mind with our continuous support and coordination.

If the initial match doesn't meet your expectations, we'll keep introducing companions until you find the right fit.
