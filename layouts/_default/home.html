How it Works

1. Consultation

A consultation with our advisor to discuss your situation and needs.

2. Matching Process

Our team will identify a companion based on shared interests, daily schedules, location, and other relevant preferences.

3. Email Introduction

Within 4 days of consultation, you'll receive an email introduction to the companion, carefully vetted and matched to suit your preferences.

4. Meeting

A meeting between you and your chosen potential homesharer to explore compatibility and get acquainted.

5. Ongoing Support & Coordination

Your selected companion extends 15-20 hours of camaraderie and assistance with chores every week. Enjoy the peace of mind knowing that our team is here to provide continuous support and coordination. If the initial match doesn't meet your expectations, we'll continue making introductions until you find the perfect fit.
